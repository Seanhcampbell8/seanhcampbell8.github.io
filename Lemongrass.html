Fox King's Lemongrass Bars

This fragrance is a duplication of the very hot Bath and Body Works fragrance. Originally inspired by a traditional Thailand fragrance, this blend is a mix of zesty lemongrass and fruity pineapple. With middle notes of lily of the valley and peach to temper the citrus, the blend ends beautifully in a smooth finish of coconut and sandalwood. This fragrance soaps a nice creamy ivory.
